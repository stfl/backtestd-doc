Metrics

:CUSTOM_ID: metrics

A metric or also called a fitness function in this system describes a function to evaluate the performance of an indicator or an Indicator Set.

Metrics:

  • Hit Miss Ratio
  • Double Hit Ratio
  • Return of Investment (ROI)
  • Profit Factor (PF)
  • Avg. Profit per Trade
  • K-Ratio
